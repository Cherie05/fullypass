Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and services

How can I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any course by visiting our pricing page and selecting the plan that suits you best. Payment can be made through various methods including credit/debit cards and UPI.

Do you offer free trial classes?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for all our courses. You can access sample lectures and materials to get a feel for our teaching methodology.

What if I need technical support?

Our technical support team is available 24/7 to help you with any issues. You can reach us through live chat, email, or phone support.

Can I switch between courses?

Yes, you can upgrade to any higher-tier course at any time. The price difference will be adjusted, and you'll get immediate access to the new course materials.
